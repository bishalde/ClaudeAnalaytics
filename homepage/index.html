How It Works

Three steps. Thirty seconds.

Your Claude Code data is already on your machine. We just help you see it.

1

Open your terminal

Works on macOS, Linux, and WSL. Just open any terminal where you use Claude Code.

2

Run the command

Paste npx claude-code-usage-analytics and press Enter. No install, no config, no API keys needed.

3

See your dashboard

Your browser opens automatically with a full breakdown of your token usage, insights, and patterns.

Features

Everything you need to understand your usage.

Built by Claude Code power users who kept hitting the limit.

Usage Breakdown

See every conversation ranked by token consumption. Discover which sessions are eating your quota with detailed per-message breakdowns.

Smart Insights

Get actionable tips personalized to your usage. Find out if vague prompts, long sessions, or model choices are costing you extra tokens.

Daily Patterns

Visualize your usage over time with daily charts. Spot trends, identify heavy days, and understand how your habits affect token consumption.

Project Analytics

Break down usage by project. See which codebases consume the most tokens and drill into the most expensive prompts per project.

Model Comparison

See token usage split by model - Opus, Sonnet, Haiku. Understand if you're using the right model for the right task.

100% Private

Everything runs locally. Your data never leaves your machine. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud. Just you and your terminal.
